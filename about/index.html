About me

I am a graduate neuroscientist who fell in love with code. My original interest came from AI and neural networks but I quickly became interested in the web. I have since worked several years as a fullstack developer. My favourite languages are python and javascript.

I like coding things that I am the user of. In June 2015, I left my job to go on a mission for one year to do the following:

  • Code at least 1 web/mobile app which generates some income.
  • Complete at least 12 MOOCs on coursera or udacity.
  • Contribute to at least 1 big open source project.
  • Create at least 1 successful open source project.
  • All while travelling South America.

I am originally from London and currently living in Lagoa da Conceição, Florianopolis, Brazil.

I am currently only considering fully remote roles or relocating to California.
